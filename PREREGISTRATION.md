# Pre-registration (frozen 2026-06-15, before real-data runs)

This study was pre-registered before touching real traces or RocksDB. Hypotheses, decision rules,
statistics, and the success ladder were fixed in advance; pre-data amendments are allowed only as
transparent struck-through edits. The full frozen document lives in the authors' research trail; the
binding claims are reproduced here.

## Constants (derived, frozen)
- Bloom: `f = exp(-β b)`, `β = ln²2 ≈ 0.4805`, `b` = bits/key.
- Objective `C(b) = Σ_i a_i exp(-β b_i)`, budget `Σ_i N_i b_i ≤ M`.
- Static optimum (Monkey): `f_i* = λ N_i / a_i`  ⇒  `b_i* = (ln a_i − ln N_i − ln λ)/β`.

## Pre-registered hypotheses & decision rules
- **H0 (gate).** (a) Monkey static optimum cuts read cost ≥30% vs uniform under skew; (b) **log-law**:
  fitted `b_i*` vs `ln a_i` is affine with slope `1/β = 2.081 ± 10%`, R² ≥ 0.98.
- **H1 (robustness law).** (a) excess read cost is linear in `D` = N-weighted variance of the log
  hotness-misestimate (i.e. **quadratic-in-log**), R² ≥ 0.90, with derived slope `1/2`; (b) a
  **common-factor** misestimate (D=0) incurs <5% of a differential one at matched `|ln κ|`.
- **H2 (adaptivity-value frontier, core).** With `V = (C_compact_only − C_oracle)/(C_uniform − C_oracle)`
  and within-epoch differential log-drift `r`: (a) a coarse-suffices regime exists (`V ≤ 0.1` below a
  boundary, rising to ≥0.5); (b) **small-drift law** `V ≈ c (r/r*)²`, `c ≈ 1/2`, R² ≥ 0.85, with
  `r* = √(2 (C_uniform − C_oracle)/C_oracle)` predicted within 2×.
- **H2c (real-trace transfer).** On ≥3 real traces, the law predicts measured `V` within ±0.15 (MAE).
- **H3 (real engine).** On RocksDB, the empirical FPR follows `exp(-β b)` in the operating range; the
  Monkey direction (fewer bits to the largest level) saves memory.
- **Negative controls.** NC1: stationary workload ⇒ `V ≈ 0` (no harness leak). NC2: `C*` monotone in `M`.
